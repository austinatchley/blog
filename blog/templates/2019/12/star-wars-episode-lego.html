LEGO is the best. Ever since I was 5, I've been obsessed with it, and my mother has fueled this passion with generous christmas gifts. Every year, if I'm good, I get a big set to build and continue my collection.


I usually build the set on the magical day itself, and I am pretty sure my mother gets a lot out of this. So, this year, she was rather sad when I brought my set back from Paris to the city by the bay.



I therefore decided to share the experience in a new way. Since it's a work of art, I'm also sharing it with you guys. Here goes, my submission to the hall of fame, or the Oscars:
